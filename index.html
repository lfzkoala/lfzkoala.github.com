Research Interests: I am interested in all aspects of modern cryptography, and more broadly theoretical computer science and security and privacy.

Short Bio: I'm a research engineer at Cheetah Mobile. Before that, I obtained my M.S. in Security Informatics from Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Abhishek Jain (1-semester capstone project advisor). Prior to this, I held a B.S. in Mathematics (Information Security Class) from Shandong University, China, advised by Prof. Xiaoyun Wang and Prof. Mingqiang Wang. During my M.S. study, I have interned at IBM Research, hosted by Dr.David Kravitz (now at DarkMatter LLC., the inventor of Digital Signature Algorithm) and Dr.Dulce Ponceleon and visited Simons Institute at UC Berkeley (Cryptography Boot Camp).
